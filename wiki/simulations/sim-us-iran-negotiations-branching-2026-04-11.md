# Branching Scenarios of the April 11 Islamabad US-Iran Talks

## Why This Simulation

The existing [[simulations/sim-what-if-us-iran-ceasefire-negotiations-succeed-or-collapse-h-2026-04-11|8-round Xi-Framework simulation]] traced **one trajectory** — an interim deal that evolves into managed Chinese custody of Iran's HEU. This follow-up explicitly **branches** across 4 scenarios with probabilities and walks each through to what matters to a Korean investor: KOSPI sectors, FX, and crypto.

- **Date of writing**: 2026-04-11 — day the Serena hotel talks between Mohammad Baqer [[iran|Qalibaf]] and VP J.D. Vance began, brokered by [[pakistan|Field-Marshal Munir]].
- **Editorial position of The Economist** (source article): interim deal is "likeliest"; grand bargain "unimaginable in two weeks"; collapse would hit "the global economy, Vance's 2028 aspirations, and the Iranian negotiators themselves."
- **Core macro facts pinned** as of today: US March CPI 3.3% (from 2.4%), Brent spot ≈ $94 (physical grades at $150 peak), Ras Laffan -17% capacity, Al Taweelah down ~1 year, 400kg HEU unaccounted, South Korean gas reserves "days from exhaustion."

## Scenario A — Grand Bargain (~10%)

### Geopolitical Path
Vance and Qalibaf use the absence of [[iran|Khamenei]] to negotiate without intermediaries — the structural unlock flagged in the Economist article. A JCPOA-plus framework emerges within 8-10 weeks: IAEA re-enters Fordow, 400kg HEU shipped to Kazakhstan or Chinese custody under IAEA seal, enrichment capped at 3.67%, missile range voluntarily limited, and all pre-war sanctions lifted in phases. [[gulf-states|MBS]] normalizes with Tehran; Israel gets an explicit US security guarantee as offset. [[donald-trump|Trump]] claims the deal, Vance banks it for 2028. [[china|Xi]] co-signs as guarantor at the May summit.

**Triggers to watch**: Iran releases Americans held in Evin, US confirms $6bn Qatar unfreeze + signals broader relief, Israel accepts 60-day Lebanon freeze.

### South Korea Impact
- **Immediate**: KOSPI gaps up +4% on announcement; Won rallies to 1,380 then 1,310 as current-account stress reverses.
- **Chips**: Samsung/SK Hynix multiple re-rate hard — AI capex cycle intact, helium from a reopening Ras Laffan flows by Q4, bromine from Israel secured as a side effect of Lebanon de-escalation. 3nm/2nm roadmap unblocked. [[south-korea|Yongin cluster]] Phase 1 accelerates; equipment suppliers (HPSP, Wonik IPS, PSK) outperform chaebols.
- **Refiners/petrochem**: S-Oil / SK Innovation / Lotte Chem rally on margin normalization, then give back gains as crack spreads compress with oil at $65.
- **LNG importers** (KOGAS): immediate relief on spot cargo costs; long-term $10bn US LNG contract signed under emergency is now "over-hedged" → political pushback.
- **Shipping & aviation**: HMM, Korean Air, Asiana rally +20-30% on lower bunker costs and insurance.
- **Defense** (Hanwha Aero, KAI, LIG Nex1): give back the 2025-26 war premium. Still structurally strong on European orders but cyclical top.
- **Jeonse/housing**: Lee administration's [[south-korea|"productive finance"]] pivot suddenly works — mortgage rates drop, chonsei landlords unwound gracefully.
- **Growth/CPI**: 2027 growth revised +0.4pp, CPI back under 2.5%.

### Global Equity Impact
- **S&P 500 → 6,400** by year-end. Tech, consumer discretionary, airlines, homebuilders lead. Energy sector craters.
- **Europe**: DAX outperforms on lower gas, but defense (CSG, Rheinmetall, Dassault) roll over — [[european-defence-industry|the Economist's CSG story]] marks the top.
- **Japan**: Nikkei lags; stronger yen hurts exporters. [[japanese-auto-industry|Honda/Toyota]] get relief but EV/China structural problems remain.
- **EM**: India rupee reverses 94.65 → 89; Indian equities +12%. Turkey, Egypt benefit from Suez volume recovery.
- **Bonds**: 10y UST to 3.7% as inflation expectations collapse. Steepener trade wins. Credit spreads tighten; [[financial-markets-2026|private credit]] stress eases — Blackstone, KKR bounce ~40% off lows.
- **Dollar**: DXY -6%. Carry trades to MXN, BRL re-engage.

### Crypto Impact
- **BTC → $118k** (+40% from today's $85k): double tailwind — risk-on AND USD weakness. 
- **ETH > BTC**: altseason revival, ETH/BTC ratio +30%. Solana rallies on Asian stablecoin flows resuming normal circuits.
- **Stablecoins**: USDT/USDC supply expands $40bn in 6 months; the war-driven "dollarization via USDT" in Iran/Turkey/Egypt partially reverses as real USD access returns.
- **Mining**: hashprice up on BTC price; Iranian illicit mining (~4-7% of global hashrate) formally regularized under sanctions relief — short-term supply overhang.
- **Risk**: a real grand bargain may accelerate US stablecoin legislation that favors USDC over USDT (Tether's sanctioned-state revenue shrinks).

### Who Wins, Who Loses (A)
- **Korea winners**: chip equipment, shipping, airlines, cosmetics (K-beauty benefits from consumer recovery), KB/Shinhan on margin normalization.
- **Korea losers**: Hanwha Aerospace, KAI, nuclear pure-plays (Doosan Enerbility), LNG terminal operators.
- **Global winners**: airlines, homebuilders, EM equities, ETH, USDC issuer (Circle).
- **Global losers**: energy majors, defense, gold miners, Tether's sanctioned-flow revenue.

## Scenario B — Interim Deal → Modus Vivendi (~55%, Base Case)

### Geopolitical Path
The outline in the Economist's April 11 piece: Iran commits to a bounded ship count through Hormuz at sub-$200k tolls, US unfreezes $6bn in Qatar, two-week ceasefire rolls into four weeks, into eight. Nuclear issue **deferred** — the "Gaza ceasefire trap" the Gulf diplomats fear. [[china|Xi Framework]] at the May summit formalizes Chinese custody of HEU as a face-saving middle path. Hormuz traffic recovers to ~55 ships/day (vs 130 pre-war, 10 during blockade). This is the trajectory already modeled in the [[simulations/sim-what-if-us-iran-ceasefire-negotiations-succeed-or-collapse-h-2026-04-11|prior 8-round simulation]].

**Triggers**: Qatar $6bn unfreeze confirmed in 2 weeks; Qalibaf survives IRGC pushback; Netanyahu accepts a Lebanon "tactical pause."

### South Korea Impact
- **KOSPI → 2,750** (grinding, not ripping). Volatility elevated. Won stabilizes at 1,420-1,440 with BOK intervention credibility restored.
- **Chips**: Samsung/SK Hynix benefit from AI demand resilience; multiples don't re-rate but earnings hold. **Bromine from Israel still at risk** — every Lebanon escalation threatens supply. [[south-korea|Samsung's $5bn synthetic etch chemical investment]] (per prior sim) becomes a structural hedge; fluorine chemical suppliers (Soulbrain, FST) outperform.
- **Refiners**: trade a range. Crack spreads remain decent with oil in mid-$80s. S-Oil dividend yield ~7% attractive.
- **Defense**: Hanwha Aerospace holds gains — European orders (NATO self-arming) are structural, not Iran-linked.
- **LNG/utility**: KOGAS unwinds emergency spot cargoes slowly; Korea Gas Corp bonds trade at tight spreads.
- **Chonsei/banks**: household debt at 90% GDP remains the slow-burn. No crisis, but no release either.
- **Critical risk**: Iran's **nuclear program advances +5-8 points on the simulator's scale** while attention is on tolls. This is the hidden fuse — Korea should use the window to build bromine alternatives and LNG diversity.

### Global Equity Impact
- **S&P 500 → 5,900**, a muddle. Low VIX but narrow breadth; AI mega-cap re-concentration. Energy rolls over slowly.
- **Europe**: mixed. DAX flat, FTSE 100 up on BP/Shell holding. CSG defense names hold European order book strength.
- **Japan**: Nikkei +5%, yen weakness and [[japanese-auto-industry|Honda turnaround skepticism]] offset.
- **EM**: India recovers partially (rupee to 92), Indonesia/Malaysia outperform on palm oil + LNG.
- **Bonds**: 10y UST 4.2%, stuck in the [[financial-markets-2026|stagflation tug-of-war]]. Steepener works mildly. Private credit stress lingers — Blackstone/KKR sideways.
- **Dollar**: DXY flat to mildly weaker. EM FX stabilizes but no rally.

### Crypto Impact
- **BTC → $92k**, the worst risk-adjusted scenario. No tail-risk bid, no risk-on thrust. Range-bound $80-95k with declining vol.
- **ETH**: flat against BTC. No altseason.
- **Stablecoins**: the real action — USDT supply continues to grow $2-3bn/month on **Iranian, Turkish, Egyptian, and Nigerian use**. The war taught emerging market users that bank accounts freeze faster than USDT wallets. Tether's profit run rate holds at >$10bn annualized.
- **Regulation**: US stablecoin bill passes; USDC gains share in the US, USDT retains sanctioned/grey-market dominance.
- **Bitcoin ETF flows**: modest net inflows ~$500m/month, nothing dramatic.
- **Risk**: bored markets, mean-reverting vol — **selling covered calls on BTC at $95k resistance** is the high-win-rate trade in this regime.

### Who Wins, Who Loses (B)
- **Korea winners**: chip equipment, fluorine chemicals (Korean bromine replacement), KOGAS bonds.
- **Korea losers**: no sharp losers; slow bleed in consumer retail, airlines underperform on range-bound oil.
- **Global winners**: Tether franchise, select EM equities, AI mega-cap re-concentration.
- **Global losers**: crypto vol sellers eventually (vol is mean-reverting — someone gets caught).

## Scenario C — Talks Collapse → Limited Strikes (~25%)

### Geopolitical Path
Iran walks on preconditions (Lebanon pause breaks, Israel strikes Nasrallah's successor in south Beirut), OR the frozen-asset demand escalates, OR a hardliner IRGC commander sinks a US-flagged vessel. Trump cycles through threat → strike. Limited US-Israeli strikes on Fordow (bunker busters, not nuclear), Natanz, and IRGC command. Iran responds with full Hormuz closure, mining of shipping lanes, and missile volleys at Gulf infrastructure. Back to a [[iran-war-and-ceasefire-2026|Phase 2 war]] — shorter but more intense.

**Triggers**: Qatar unfreeze stalls > 3 weeks; IAEA emergency report confirms 90%+ enrichment before deal locks; Israel strikes Lebanon target Iran can't ignore.

### South Korea Impact
- **KOSPI → 2,100** (-18%). Intraday limit-down sessions possible. Won spikes to **1,560-1,620**; BOK intervenes, burns $30-40bn of reserves.
- **Chips — existential**: helium inventories run out by August; bromine from Israel fully cut; 3nm production delayed 4-6 months. **Samsung/SK Hynix 2026 EPS revised -25% to -40%**. The [[south-korea|Yongin mega-cluster]] becomes a political liability — "why are we spending $530bn when we can't even source bromine?"
- **Refiners**: short-term margin blowout → then demand destruction; net negative beyond 8 weeks.
- **Defense**: **massive outperformance**. Hanwha Aerospace +60%, KAI +50%, LIG Nex1 +70%. Government approves KF-21 export fast-track.
- **Jeonse system → banking stress**: household debt at 90% GDP + rates repriced higher + Won collapse = the **deposit-return rollovers break** for marginal landlords. Financial supervisory emergency measures.
- **Startups**: mass bankruptcies — the Economist flagged this (startups lack months of stock that Samsung has). Lee government emergency credit line ~2% GDP.
- **Shipbuilders**: HD Korea Shipbuilding & Offshore, Samsung Heavy — LNG carrier orderbook **triples**; US/Europe desperate for regas capacity. +40%.
- **Nuclear policy shift**: Lee government quietly commissions study on weapons-latency. Public signal that Korea's 20% enrichment capability is "an insurance policy."

### Global Equity Impact
- **S&P 500 → 4,950** (-12%). VIX spikes 45+. AI mega-cap breaks correlation (exempt from war tail, but giving back multiple). Energy +35%. Airlines -40%. Homebuilders -20% on rate volatility.
- **Europe**: DAX -18% on gas panic; defense (CSG, Rheinmetall, Saab) +50%; European banks hit hard on private credit stress.
- **Japan**: Nikkei -15%. Autos -25%. Korean defense outperforms Japanese.
- **EM**: India rupee → 98. Indian equities -20%. Turkey, Egypt hammered on fertilizer + oil double-hit.
- **Bonds**: 10y UST spikes to 4.8% on inflation panic, then **collapses to 4.3%** as recession fear takes over. Stagflation whipsaw maximum amplitude. **Private credit crisis**: Blue Owl redemption gates reactivate, Blackstone/KKR -30%, secondary market discounts blow out to 30-40%.
- **Dollar**: DXY +6% initially on safe-haven, then +3% as Fed cuts signaled.
- **Gold → $3,150** (+17%).

### Crypto Impact
This is where crypto gets interesting — the narrative wins.
- **BTC**: **initial 15-20% dump on leverage liquidations** to $70-72k → V-shape rally to **$96k by year-end** (flat for year). Net story: digital gold thesis validated.
- **ETH**: underperforms BTC initially (higher beta), ETH/BTC -20% in the drawdown.
- **Stablecoins**: **explosive demand**. USDT supply +$25bn in 8 weeks. Iran, Russia, and sanctioned-state commerce runs entirely on Tron-USDT. US Treasury threatens secondary sanctions on Tether → partial freeze on certain addresses → but the franchise adapts.
- **Circle/USDC**: US officials promote as "compliant alternative"; USDC market share in US/EU grows to 45% (from 22%).
- **Mining**: **Iranian hashrate offline** during strikes (grid hit) — global difficulty adjusts down 8%, bonanza for Texas/Paraguay miners.
- **Gold-vs-BTC**: gold wins the flight-to-safety trade in week 1; BTC wins the 6-month trade.
- **Altcoins**: wipeout. Most alts -50-70%. Memecoins worse.
- **Key trade**: **long BTC/gold basket, short alts, short Tether corporate risk**.

### Who Wins, Who Loses (C)
- **Korea winners**: Hanwha Aerospace, KAI, LIG Nex1, HD Korea Shipbuilding, fluorine chemical companies, defense-linked specialty materials.
- **Korea losers**: Samsung, SK Hynix (short-term), airlines, shipping (Hormuz), midsize chip fabless, chonsei-exposed regional banks, startups broadly.
- **Global winners**: US/European defense, LNG infrastructure, BTC (6-month), gold, Tether (operationally, despite US pressure).
- **Global losers**: altcoins, airlines, autos, Japanese exporters, EM currencies ex-LNG exporters, private credit holders.

## Scenario D — Nuclear Breakout / Preemptive Strike (~10%, Tail)

### Geopolitical Path
IAEA report in July or August confirms 90%+ enrichment approaching weapons-grade assembly. Israel strikes unilaterally (F-35I + bunker busters) OR the US crosses its own red line and hits Fordow directly. Iran declares a Pakistan-style "nuclear state" status. Saudi Arabia announces enrichment program within 30 days. India pivots toward Iran (already there, per [[india]] rupee crisis + China rapprochement). NPT effectively dead. This is the **Blundering to Armageddon** scenario our [[great-power-rivalry|wiki has already flagged]].

**Triggers**: Iran denies inspectors for >45 days; Israeli F-35 squadron movements to Nevatim; China publicly warns against US strike (signal rather than deterrent).

### South Korea Impact
- **KOSPI → 1,800** (-30%). Emergency short-sale ban. Circuit breakers daily. Won → 1,720 despite BOK intervention.
- **The jeonse crisis triggers**: mortgage rates repriced + Won + inflation = **bank run risk at regional lenders**. Financial Services Commission emergency deposit insurance boost. Household debt 90% GDP becomes a live crisis.
- **Chips**: 6-month production freeze on high-end nodes. Global AI capex cycle pauses. Samsung EPS -55%, SK Hynix -60%. Yongin Phase 1 put on hold.
- **Nuclear latency → declared capability**: Korea's 20% nuclear enrichment capability becomes **explicit policy**. NPT withdrawal debate begins publicly. Japan follows.
- **Structural winners**: Hanwha Aero, KAI, HD HHI/Samsung Heavy (LNG), nuclear plant builders (Doosan Enerbility), fluorine chemical.
- **Defense spending**: 3% → 4.5% of GDP by 2028.
- **Recession**: -2.5% GDP in 2026. Biggest shock since 1998 IMF crisis.

### Global Equity Impact
- **S&P 500 → 4,400** (-22%). Full bear market. VIX 55+. Circuit breakers.
- **Europe**: DAX -25%. Defense +80%. Energy +60%.
- **Japan**: Nikkei -25%.
- **EM**: total rout. Rupee → 105. Indian equities -35%.
- **Bonds**: 10y UST spikes to 5.0% → collapses to 3.8% as Fed signals emergency cuts. Yield curve bull-steepens violently.
- **Dollar**: DXY +10% initially (classic flight-to-safety) → then weakens as Fed slashes. Over 6 months DXY flat.
- **Gold → $3,600** (+34%). Central bank buying accelerates (already a trend; [[china]] and Gulf pile in further).

### Crypto Impact
This is the **most interesting crypto scenario** — bifurcated outcomes.
- **BTC**: crash -30% to $60k in 72 hours on leverage unwind → **V-shape to $105k** (+24% from today) within 6 months as sovereign reserve diversification accelerates. El Salvador's 2021 bet looks prophetic.
- **ETH**: underperforms BTC by 40pp. Solana, alts -60-80%.
- **Stablecoins**: **existential moment**. Sanctioned-state stablecoin use (Iran, Russia, Venezuela, possibly Saudi proxies) becomes the majority of USDT volume. US Treasury Department either:
  - (a) crushes Tether (BTC +15% on panic),
  - (b) reaches accommodation (stabilizes), or
  - (c) licenses a "national stablecoin" via Circle + a major bank.
  Scenario (c) is most likely given TradFi lobbying. **USDC share → 60%** in Western markets.
- **Bitcoin spot ETFs**: **massive inflows** once stabilized — sovereign wealth funds and pension funds add 1-3% allocation. Norges Bank, ADIA, GIC quietly buy.
- **Mining**: Iranian hashrate gone. Texas/Paraguay/Bhutan pick up slack but difficulty adjusts down 12%.
- **Custodial regulation**: Korea's Virtual Asset User Protection Act proves prescient; domestic won-pair volumes spike as retail seeks inflation hedge; **upbit/bithumb handle 2-3x normal flow**.
- **Korean crypto retail**: historically outsized "kimchi premium" returns. Expect 8-15% premium on BTC during peak panic weeks.

### Who Wins, Who Loses (D)
- **Korea winners**: Hanwha, KAI, Doosan Enerbility, shipbuilders, fluorine chemical, **crypto exchanges (Upbit/Bithumb)** on volume.
- **Korea losers**: almost everyone else; this is a recession scenario.
- **Global winners**: gold, defense, BTC (6-month), Circle, US Treasury bonds (6-month), LNG infrastructure.
- **Global losers**: altcoins, autos, airlines, private credit (gates everywhere), EM ex-LNG, Japan.

## Korean Investor Playbook

### Retail (KRW-denominated portfolio)
1. **Barbell the portfolio**: 50% defensive (KODEX 200, dividend ETF, KOGAS-type utility, short-duration KTB), 35% quality growth (Samsung, SK Hynix, chip equipment), 15% tail hedges.
2. **Tail hedges for KRW investor**:
   - Long USD exposure (5-10% via dollar deposit, US Treasury ETF, or USD-hedged S&P) — protects the left tail.
   - Long gold (KRX gold 2-5%) — pays in C and D, costs little in A/B.
   - **BTC 1-3% position**: asymmetric. Loses limited in B; pays in A (risk-on); pays in C/D (sovereign diversification + stablecoin dollar access).
3. **Chonsei exposure**: if you hold a chonsei deposit in a marginal landlord's property, **actively renegotiate to monthly rent** before Scenario C materializes. This is the least-discussed left-tail risk in Korean household finance.
4. **Sector avoid list**: leveraged REITs, chonsei-funded landlords, midsize chip fabless without helium hedges, airlines with high fuel beta and no hedges.
5. **Actively monitor**: Qatar $6bn unfreeze status (binary trigger for Scenario B→A pivot); IAEA statements; Israel-Lebanon flare-ups.

### Institutional (KRW institutional mandate)
- **Scenario-conditional overlays**: buy KOSPI 200 puts 5% OTM as insurance (~1.5% premium/quarter) — pays in C/D.
- **Long defense basket** (Hanwha Aero / KAI / LIG Nex1 equal-weighted): pays in B, C, D; only underperforms in A. Risk/reward attractive.
- **Pair trade**: long SK Hynix / short Japanese auto basket (Toyota, Honda, Nissan). Korea chip exceeds Japan auto in every scenario except a 2028 EV China disruption.
- **FX**: underweight Won vs USD; overweight Won vs JPY.
- **Credit**: avoid Korean private credit / mezzanine exposure — the 90% household debt overhang means Scenario C becomes a credit event.

### Crypto-Specific
- **Core long BTC** (50-70% of crypto allocation): wins in A, C, D; neutral in B.
- **Tactical ETH** (10-25%): overweight only if Scenario A probability rises (sanctions relief → stablecoin legal clarity → DeFi summer).
- **Stablecoin stack**: **hold USDC not USDT** for Korean users (regulatory risk); use USDT only for on-ramp/off-ramp speed.
- **Upbit/Bithumb KRW pair trading**: the kimchi premium widens in tail scenarios — a natural domestic arbitrage / retail hedge.
- **Avoid**: memecoins, long-tail alts, leveraged perps into a known binary catalyst (the Serena talks, IAEA reports).
- **Watchlist**: stablecoin legislation in US, Korean FSC crypto ETF approval path (currently blocked; a Scenario A tailwind would accelerate it).

## Uncertainties and Caveats

- **Vance's 2028 incentive alignment is the strongest pro-deal force** — but also means Vance could over-promise and under-deliver.
- **Israel is the outsider spoiler**: Netanyahu's October 2026 election creates a perverse incentive for controlled escalation.
- **China is the structural winner in B, C, and D** — in A, it loses relative influence. This is why Xi may actually **prefer** a B/C outcome to an A outcome. Watch for Chinese actions that quietly sabotage the grand bargain.
- **The nuclear clock is the hidden variable**: every week of delay in getting IAEA into Fordow shifts probability mass from A/B toward D.
- **Crypto numbers have wider error bars** than traditional assets — BTC ranges above are ±30%, not ±10%.
- **The [[south-korea|Korean jeonse/household debt]] situation is a multiplier**: any currency/rate shock gets amplified. This is the feature that distinguishes 2026 from 1997 — household rather than corporate leverage.

# 미-이란 협상: 분기 시나리오와 한국/글로벌 시장 플레이북

## 이 시뮬레이션을 만든 이유

기존의 [[simulations/sim-what-if-us-iran-ceasefire-negotiations-succeed-or-collapse-h-2026-04-11|8라운드 Xi-Framework 시뮬레이션]]은 **하나의 궤적만** 추적했다 — 잠정합의가 이란 HEU의 중국 관리로 진화하는 경로. 이 후속편은 확률을 명시한 **4개 시나리오로 분기**하여, 한국 투자자 관점(KOSPI 섹터, 환율, 암호화폐)으로 각각을 풀어낸다.

- **작성일**: 2026-04-11 — [[iran|칼리바프]](Qalibaf) 이란 의회 의장과 밴스 미국 부통령의 Serena 호텔 회담이 시작된 날. [[pakistan|무니르 육군 원수]] 중재.
- **원 기사(The Economist)의 입장**: 잠정합의가 "가장 유력"; 2주 안에 포괄적 합의는 "상상할 수 없음"; 결렬 시 "세계경제, 밴스의 2028년 대선 야망, 이란 협상단 본인들"이 피해를 입음.
- **오늘 기준 확정된 거시 지표**: 3월 미국 CPI 3.3%(2월 2.4%에서 상승), 브렌트 현물 ≈ $94(피크 시 실제 물리 거래는 $150), 라스 라판 용량 -17%, 알 타윌라 약 1년 정지, 400kg HEU 행방 불명, 한국 가스 비축 "고갈 직전."

## 시나리오 A — 포괄적 대타결 (~10%)

### 지정학 경로
밴스와 칼리바프가 [[iran|하메네이]] 공백을 활용해 중간자 없이 직접 협상한다 — Economist 기사가 지적한 구조적 해금. 8-10주 내 JCPOA+ 프레임워크 등장: IAEA의 Fordow 재진입, 400kg HEU를 카자흐스탄 혹은 중국 관리하 IAEA 봉인으로 이전, 농축률 3.67% 상한, 미사일 사거리 자발적 제한, 전쟁 전 제재 단계적 해제. [[gulf-states|MBS]]는 테헤란과 정상화; 이스라엘에는 명시적 미국 안보보장 제공. [[donald-trump|트럼프]]는 성과로 선전, 밴스는 2028을 위해 은행처럼 예치. 5월 정상회담에서 [[china|시진핑]]이 보증인으로 공동서명.

**트리거**: Evin 감옥의 미국인 석방, 카타르 60억 달러 해빙 + 광범위 제재 완화 신호, 이스라엘의 60일 레바논 중단 수용.

### 한국 영향
- **즉각 반응**: KOSPI 발표 즉시 +4% 갭 상승; 원화는 1,380으로, 이어 1,310까지 강세 전환(경상수지 스트레스 해소).
- **반도체**: Samsung/SK Hynix 멀티플 대폭 재평가 — AI 설비투자 사이클 유지, 라스 라판 재가동으로 4분기 헬륨 공급 복귀, 이스라엘로부터의 브로민도 레바논 디에스컬레이션의 부수효과로 확보. 3nm/2nm 로드맵 활성화. [[south-korea|용인 클러스터]] 1단계 가속; 장비 협력사(HPSP, 원익IPS, PSK)가 대기업을 상회.
- **정유·석화**: S-Oil / SK이노베이션 / 롯데케미칼이 마진 정상화로 랠리, 이후 유가가 $65로 내려가며 크랙스프레드 축소로 되돌림.
- **LNG 수입사(KOGAS)**: 즉각적 스팟 카고 비용 부담 완화; 비상시 체결한 100억 달러 규모 미국 LNG 장기계약이 "헤지 초과" 상태 → 정치적 역풍.
- **해운·항공**: HMM, 대한항공, 아시아나 연료비 및 보험료 하락으로 +20-30%.
- **방산**(한화에어로스페이스, KAI, LIG넥스원): 2025-26년 전쟁 프리미엄 반납. 유럽 수주 기조는 유지되나 경기적 고점.
- **전세·부동산**: 이재명 정부의 [[south-korea|"생산적 금융"]] 정책이 급작스레 효과 — 주택담보대출 금리 하락, 한계 전세 임대인의 디레버리징이 질서 있게 진행.
- **성장·물가**: 2027년 성장률 +0.4%p 상향, CPI 2.5% 미만 복귀.

### 글로벌 주식 영향
- **S&P 500 → 6,400** (연말). 기술·경기소비재·항공·주택건설주 주도. 에너지 섹터 급락.
- **유럽**: 가스 가격 하락으로 DAX 초과수익, 그러나 방산(CSG, Rheinmetall, Dassault) 하락 — [[european-defence-industry|Economist의 CSG 기사]]가 고점 신호로 회고됨.
- **일본**: Nikkei 부진. 엔 강세가 수출 타격. [[japanese-auto-industry|혼다·토요타]]는 원자재 관점에서만 유리하고 EV·중국 구조 문제는 해결되지 않음.
- **신흥국**: 인도 루피 94.65 → 89으로 반전; 인도 주식 +12%. 튀르키예·이집트는 수에즈 물동량 회복 수혜.
- **채권**: 10년물 UST 3.7%, 인플레이션 기대 붕괴. 스티프너 거래 승리. 크레딧 스프레드 축소; [[financial-markets-2026|사모대출]] 스트레스 완화 — 블랙스톤·KKR 저점 대비 ~40% 반등.
- **달러**: DXY -6%. MXN, BRL 캐리 트레이드 재개.

### 암호화폐 영향
- **BTC → $118k** (오늘 $85k 대비 +40%): 위험자산 온 + USD 약세의 이중 순풍.
- **ETH > BTC**: 알트시즌 재점화, ETH/BTC 비율 +30%. 솔라나도 아시아 스테이블코인 흐름 정상화로 랠리.
- **스테이블코인**: USDT/USDC 공급이 6개월간 400억 달러 증가; 이란·튀르키예·이집트 "USDT 달러화" 일부 되돌림(실제 달러 접근 복귀).
- **채굴**: BTC 가격 상승으로 해시프라이스 반등; 이란 불법 채굴(~글로벌 해시율 4-7%)이 제재 완화로 공식화 → 단기 공급 증가 영향.
- **리스크**: 진짜 대타결이면 미국 스테이블코인 입법 가속 → USDC에 유리, USDT 제재국 수익 축소 가능성.

### 승자와 패자 (A)
- **한국 승자**: 반도체 장비, 해운, 항공, 화장품(K-뷰티 소비 회복 수혜), KB·신한(마진 정상화).
- **한국 패자**: 한화에어로, KAI, 원전 순수 플레이(두산에너빌리티), LNG 터미널 운영사.
- **글로벌 승자**: 항공주, 주택건설주, EM 주식, ETH, USDC 발행사(Circle).
- **글로벌 패자**: 에너지 메이저, 방산, 금광주, Tether의 제재-경로 매출.

## 시나리오 B — 잠정합의 → 관리된 공존 (~55%, 베이스 케이스)

### 지정학 경로
Economist 기사가 그린 윤곽 그대로: 이란은 호르무즈를 통한 제한된 척수 통과를 $200k 미만 통행료로 약속, 미국은 카타르의 60억 달러를 해빙, 2주 휴전이 4주, 8주로 갱신. 핵 이슈는 **유예** — 걸프 외교관들이 두려워한 "가자 휴전 함정." 5월 정상회담의 [[china|Xi Framework]]이 이란 HEU의 중국 관리를 체면유지용 중도안으로 공식화. 호르무즈 통항량은 일 약 55척 회복(전쟁 전 130, 봉쇄 중 10 대비). 이 궤적이 [[simulations/sim-what-if-us-iran-ceasefire-negotiations-succeed-or-collapse-h-2026-04-11|기존 8라운드 시뮬레이션]]에서 이미 모델링됨.

**트리거**: 2주 내 카타르 60억 달러 해빙 확인; 칼리바프가 IRGC 반발을 견뎌냄; 네타냐후가 레바논 "전술적 중단"을 수용.

### 한국 영향
- **KOSPI → 2,750** (랠리가 아닌 완만한 상승). 변동성은 높음. 원화는 1,420-1,440에서 안정, BOK 개입 신뢰 회복.
- **반도체**: Samsung/SK Hynix는 AI 수요 견조 수혜; 멀티플 재평가는 없지만 실적 유지. **이스라엘발 브로민은 여전히 위험** — 레바논 긴장이 고조될 때마다 공급 위협. [[south-korea|Samsung의 50억 달러 합성 식각 화학 투자]](기존 시뮬레이션 참조)가 구조적 헤지로 작동; 불소계 화학(솔브레인, FST) 초과수익.
- **정유**: 박스권 거래. 유가 $80대 중반에서 크랙스프레드는 준수. S-Oil 배당수익률 ~7%로 매력.
- **방산**: 한화에어로 상승 유지 — 유럽 발주(NATO 자체 무장)는 이란과 무관한 구조적 수요.
- **LNG·유틸리티**: KOGAS 긴급 스팟 카고 점진적 해소; 한국가스공사 채권 타이트 스프레드.
- **전세·은행**: 가계부채 GDP 90%는 여전히 서서히 진행되는 뇌관. 위기는 없지만 해소도 없음.
- **결정적 리스크**: 이란의 **핵 프로그램이 관심이 통행료에 쏠린 사이 시뮬레이터 기준 +5~8점 진행**. 이것이 숨겨진 도화선 — 한국은 이 시간 창을 브로민 대체 및 LNG 다변화에 써야 함.

### 글로벌 주식 영향
- **S&P 500 → 5,900**, 난해한 횡보. 낮은 VIX지만 폭 좁은 상승; AI 대형주 재집중. 에너지는 완만 하락.
- **유럽**: 혼조. DAX 평탄, FTSE 100은 BP/Shell 버팀. CSG 방산은 유럽 수주 잔고 유지.
- **일본**: Nikkei +5%, 엔 약세와 [[japanese-auto-industry|혼다 턴어라운드 회의론]] 상쇄.
- **신흥국**: 인도 부분 회복(루피 92), 인도네시아·말레이시아가 팜유+LNG로 초과수익.
- **채권**: 10년물 UST 4.2%, [[financial-markets-2026|스태그플레이션 줄다리기]]에 고착. 스티프너 약간 승리. 사모대출 스트레스 잔존 — 블랙스톤·KKR 횡보.
- **달러**: DXY 평탄 내지 소폭 약세. EM FX 안정화하지만 랠리 없음.

### 암호화폐 영향
- **BTC → $92k**, 가장 위험조정 열위인 시나리오. 꼬리 리스크 비드도, 위험자산 온 추진력도 없음. $80-95k 박스권, 변동성 감소.
- **ETH**: BTC 대비 평탄. 알트시즌 없음.
- **스테이블코인**: 진짜 움직임. USDT 공급은 월 20-30억 달러 증가 지속 — **이란, 튀르키예, 이집트, 나이지리아 사용자 기반**. 전쟁이 EM 사용자들에게 "은행 계좌는 USDT 지갑보다 빨리 동결된다"는 교훈을 남김. Tether 이익 run rate 연 100억 달러 이상 유지.
- **규제**: 미국 스테이블코인 법안 통과; USDC가 미국 시장점유율 확대, USDT는 제재·회색 시장 지배력 유지.
- **비트코인 ETF 유입**: 월 5억 달러 수준 소폭 순유입. 큰 뉴스 없음.
- **리스크**: 지루한 시장, 평균회귀성 변동성 — **BTC $95k 저항선에서 커버드 콜 매도**가 이 국면의 고승률 전략.

### 승자와 패자 (B)
- **한국 승자**: 반도체 장비, 불소계 화학(한국 브로민 대체), KOGAS 채권.
- **한국 패자**: 급격한 패자는 없음; 소비재·소매 둔화, 항공은 박스권 유가에 부진.
- **글로벌 승자**: Tether 프랜차이즈, 일부 EM 주식, AI 대형주 재집중.
- **글로벌 패자**: 크립토 변동성 매도자들(결국 누군가는 변동성 평균회귀에 쓸림).

## 시나리오 C — 협상 결렬 → 제한적 공습 (~25%)

### 지정학 경로
이란이 전제조건(레바논 중단 파기, 이스라엘이 남부 베이루트의 나스랄라 후계자 타격)으로 퇴장, 혹은 동결자산 요구가 파행, 혹은 강경파 IRGC 사령관이 미국 국적 선박 격침. 트럼프는 위협 → 공습 사이클. Fordow(벙커 버스터, 비핵), 나탄즈, IRGC 지휘부에 미-이 제한 타격. 이란은 호르무즈 완전 봉쇄와 항로 기뢰 부설, 걸프 인프라 미사일 세례로 대응. [[iran-war-and-ceasefire-2026|2차 전쟁 페이즈]] — 더 짧지만 더 강렬.

**트리거**: 카타르 해빙이 3주 이상 지연; IAEA 긴급 보고서로 90%+ 농축 확인(타결 전); 이스라엘이 이란이 외면 못 할 레바논 목표 타격.

## 불확실성과 주의점

- **밴스의 2028 인센티브는 가장 강한 친-합의 동력** — 그러나 동시에 밴스가 과다 약속·과소 이행할 가능성.
- **이스라엘이 외부 스포일러**: 네타냐후의 2026년 10월 선거가 통제된 긴장 고조에 역인센티브 제공.
- **중국은 B·C·D에서 구조적 승자** — A에서는 상대적 영향력 감소. 이것이 시진핑이 실제로는 A보다 B·C를 **선호**할 이유. 포괄적 대타결을 조용히 방해하는 중국 행동 주시.
- **핵 시계가 숨은 변수**: IAEA가 Fordow에 들어가지 못하는 매 주마다 확률 질량이 A·B에서 D로 이동.
- **암호화폐 숫자는 오차범위가 더 큼** — 위 BTC 범위는 ±30%, 전통 자산의 ±10%가 아님.
- **[[south-korea|한국 전세·가계부채]] 상황이 승수(multiplier)**: 통화·금리 충격이 증폭됨. 1997년과 다른 2026년의 특징 — 기업 레버리지가 아니라 가계 레버리지.
